About Modern Surgiments

Our Legacy & Commitment

Established in 1985 as NEW INDIA SURGIMENTS, we have evolved into MODERN SURGIMENTS with over four decades of dedication to excellence in surgical equipment manufacturing. Our journey has been marked by innovation, quality craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to healthcare professionals.

We specialize in manufacturing high-quality surgical equipment and furniture that meets international standards. Our products are designed with precision engineering and tested rigorously to ensure reliability and performance in critical healthcare environments.

Our Services

Comprehensive support and specialized services to ensure optimal performance of your surgical equipment

Repair & Maintenance

We undertake repair works for all types of surgical furniture and instruments. Our skilled technicians ensure your equipment is restored to optimal working condition with genuine parts and professional expertise.

Modernization Service

We specialize in modernizing old manual ICU cots into motorized systems at minimal cost. This innovative service extends the life of your existing equipment while providing the benefits of motorized functionality.

Custom Manufacturing

We offer custom manufacturing services for surgical instruments and furniture based on specific requirements. Our team works closely with healthcare professionals to create solutions that meet exact specifications.
